About This Project

Why We Built This

In today’s competitive world, students often feel lost when selecting courses, colleges, or career paths. There is a lack of personalized, data-driven platforms that help students visualize their future academically and professionally.

Our AI-Enhanced Career Guidance System solves this by providing smart, personalized career pathways for students from 10th grade, 12th grade, UG, and PG levels. It uses AI suggestions, real data trends, and user-friendly guidance to bridge the gap between academic decisions and industry needs.

Problem Statement

Many students make poor educational choices due to limited awareness, misleading advice, or lack of proper guidance. Our system solves this by providing:

  • Clear guidance for future studies
  • Top colleges and last year’s cutoffs
  • In-demand careers and technologies
  • Expected salaries and job roles
  • AI-powered personalized suggestions
