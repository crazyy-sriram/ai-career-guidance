🔍 Sample Academic to Career Pathways

  1. 10th Standard ➔ Choose Stream (Science, Commerce, Arts, Vocational)
  2. 12th Standard ➔ Focused Subjects + Skill Building + Competitive Exams
  3. UG (Undergraduate) ➔ Specialized Degree (Engineering, BBA, BSc, etc.) + Internships + Certifications
  4. PG (Postgraduate) ➔ Master's / MBA / M.Tech / MSc / Research / PhD
  5. Career Entry ➔ Industry, Corporate, Research, Startups, Government
  6. Career Growth ➔ Leadership, Entrepreneurship, Global Opportunities

💼 Career Roadmap Tips from Experts

  • ✔️ Follow structured planning, not random choices
  • ✔️ Choose courses aligned to future job trends
  • ✔️ Gain certifications alongside your degree
  • ✔️ Build portfolio / projects for practical exposure
  • ✔️ Learn digital & leadership skills early
